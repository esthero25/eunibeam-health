About Us

Eunibeam Healthcare is an independently owned care agency with extensive experience in the health and social care sector. We are committed to delivering high-quality, person-centred care and support services to individuals living within their own homes and communities.

Our services are tailored to meet the needs of adults who, due to illness, disability, or physical frailty, require assistance with everyday activities. At Eunibeam Healthcare, we take the time to get to know each individual, ensuring that the care and support we provide is personalised, compassionate, and responsive to their unique needs. From the moment you join us, we strive to make you feel valued, supported, and part of the Eunibeam Healthcare family.
