What is a Senior Care Assistant?

A senior care assistant is a professional who provides support and assistance to elderly individuals, typically within their homes, assisted living facilities, or nursing homes.

  • Personal Care
  • Medication Management
  • Mobility Support
  • Household Tasks
  • Companionship
  • Health Monitoring

Senior care assistants play a crucial role in enhancing the quality of life for elderly individuals by offering personalized care and support.
